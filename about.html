About Us

AmeRIntegrate is a non-for-profit organization registered in New Jersey, the United States of America. The organization recognizes that immigration would continue on an upward trend in the globalized world, especially as national inequality continues un-abated. Inequality among nations is caused by unequal endowment of natural resources of occurrences of natural disasters, wars, poor managerial competencies of leaders in some countries, extended political edge and dominance by colonial masters after independence of their former colonies resulting in implicit colonization by the world powers, and the levels of socio-economic developments.
The powerful countries continue to dominate the weaker countries through inadequate use of their human (brain drains) and natural resources to enhance their operations and wealth. The weaker countries are further weakened by Unfair Trade, inadequate compensation for the use of their resources. Given that there is no end in sight for the globalized inequality, it is an acceptable phenomenon that mobility across nations would continue. In the unforeseeable future. The United Nations report 2020 showed that about 281 million people in the world live outside their countries of birth. Immigrants’ recipient governments are responsible for formulating the immigration policies to adequately cover border protection, national security, continued welfare of established citizenry and immigrants’ integration within the system. To effectively integrate immigrants, the MIPEX has outlined 8 key measures and all country’s immigrant integration activities are continually gauged against these measures. Among the scholarly literature and within the international social circles, it is agreed that immigrant recipient countries have the onerous responsibility of ensuring adequate integration of immigrants through effective policy formulation and implementation. Efficiently integrating immigrants is a win-win for both the immigrants and the recipient nation because immigrants always assist in the socio-economic development of a country and except, they are fully integrated, may not be able to fully engage their potentials and the possibility of costly social vices may be experienced by recipient governments AmeRIntegrate was established to apply a two-pronged approach of assisting immigrants in North America to fully integrate and partnering with governments to ensure better deployment of immigration integration tools for the benefit of the immigrants and the society at large.

VISION

The full and mutually beneficial integration of immigrants into host countries.

MISSION

  1. Our mission is to work strategically and diligently to bridge gaps in government immigration policies establishment and implementation for the benefit of immigrants, the established society and the government.

MISSION

  1. Our mission is to strategically and diligently enhance immigrants’ integration with the established communities in host nations through bridging the gaps between government immigration policies institutionalization and implementation on the one side , and immigrant and established society co-existence on the other hand.
